# CDD_EVIDENCE.md: Governance Evidence Log

## Capability: Risk Detection
- **Implemented Evidence**: Five core risk dimensions embedded in every step assessment.
- **Impact**: Provides a comprehensive surface area for identifying potential AI failures.

## Capability: Fragility Identification
- **Implemented Evidence**: Cascading failure analysis and AI dependency concentration scoring.
- **Impact**: Highlights single points of failure where AI over-reliance could lead to systemic collapse.

## Capability: Oversight Gap Detection
- **Implemented Evidence**: Automated alerts for AI-involved steps without human review or escalation paths.
- **Impact**: Surfaces hidden "black boxes" in workflows where human accountability is missing.

## Capability: Mitigation Planning
- **Implemented Evidence**: Structured fields for controls, safeguards, and monitoring requirements.
- **Impact**: Transforms risk identification into actionable governance improvements.

## Capability: Traceability & Auditability
- **Implemented Evidence**: Governance-ready exports in JSON, Markdown, and PDF.
- **Impact**: Provides a permanent, timestamped record of the risk assessment for compliance.
