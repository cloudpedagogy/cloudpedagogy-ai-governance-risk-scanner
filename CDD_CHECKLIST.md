# CDD_CHECKLIST.md: Capability-Driven Development Verification

## Capability Intent
- [ ] Does the tool detect risk?
- [ ] Does the tool expose fragility?
- [ ] Does the tool strengthen governance?

## Embedded Capabilities
- [ ] **Risk Awareness**: Are there explicit risk categories?
- [ ] **Accountability**: Is accountability defined for each step?
- [ ] **Traceability**: Can you trace the risk score back to specific step assessments?
- [ ] **Reviewability**: Is there a structured review/reflection layer?

## Visible Evidence
- [ ] **Risk Categories**: Bias, Operational, Ethical, Governance, Privacy.
- [ ] **Oversight Fields**: Human review present, accountability defined, escalation defined.
- [ ] **Failure Analysis**: Failure description, impact, cascade effects.
- [ ] **Mitigation Planning**: Controls, safeguards, monitoring.
- [ ] **Governance Scoring**: Overall score and risk profile.

## UX & Tone
- [ ] Is the tone analytical and structured?
- [ ] Does it avoid a "playful" or "checklist" feel?
- [ ] Does it facilitate a deep audit flow?
