# DESIGN_PRINCIPLES.md: CloudPedagogy AI Governance Risk Scanner

## Governance Rigour over Automation
The tool should prompt deep thinking and rigorous analysis. Avoid over-automating the risk assessment; instead, provide the framework for humans to identify risks.

## Analytical Clarity over Visual Complexity
The UI should be clean, professional, and audit-like. Information density should be managed to focus on critical risk areas and oversight gaps.

## Inspectability over Abstraction
Users should always be able to see the data and logic behind the risk scores. Exported reports must be clear and easily auditable.

## Professional Audit Feel
The aesthetic should evoke a sense of trust and professionalism. Use a structured, grid-based layout, clear typography (Inter/Roboto), and a restrained but meaningful color palette (e.g., semantic colors for risk levels).

## Local-First & Privacy-First
Data never leaves the browser. This is non-negotiable for governance and risk data.

## Capability-Driven Design
Ensure every feature maps back to a core governance capability:
- **Risk Awareness**: Explicit categories.
- **Accountability**: Clear oversight fields.
- **Traceability**: Step-by-step analysis.
- **Reviewability**: Structured exports.
